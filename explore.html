Explore Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar (Aurangabad)

Bibi Ka Maqbara

Bibi Ka Maqbara – Known as the “Baby Taj Mahal,” this 17th-century mausoleum was built by Prince Azam Shah in memory of his mother. Its elegant Mughal architecture and serene gardens make it a must-visit, located just ~6 km from the city center.

Ellora Caves

Ellora Caves – A UNESCO World Heritage Site with 34 rock-cut temples showcasing Buddhist, Hindu, and Jain traditions. The site spans centuries (6th–10th century), with the magnificent Kailasa Temple as its highlight. It lies around ~30 km from the city. Its intricate carvings and sheer scale make it a masterpiece of ancient Indian rock-cut architecture.

Grishneshwar Templet

Also known as Ghrishneshwar or Dhushmeshwar Temple, is a revered Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva and is one of the 12 Jyotirlingas. It is located in Verul village, near Ellora Caves and Aurangabad, Maharashtra, India. The temple is renowned for its intricate carvings and is considered the 12th and final Jyotirlinga.

Panchakki & Aurangabad Caves

Panchakki & Aurangabad Caves – Within the city limits, visit the tranquil Panchakki water mill (~5 km) and the ancient Aurangabad Caves (~9 km), carved into rock and echoing Buddhist legacy. Ideal for peaceful and reflective exploration.

Ajanta Caves

📍 Ajanta Caves (Approx. 100 km | 2.5 hrs drive)

Daulatabad Fort

🏰 Daulatabad Fort (Approx. 16 km)

⏱ Plan 1.5–2 hours for a full climb | 👟 Wear walking shoes!

Key Industrial Zones Around Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar

🔷 Shendra MIDC & AURIC (Shendra–Bidkin Industrial Area)

A modern 902-hectare greenfield industrial estate that is part of the Delhi–Mumbai Industrial Corridor project. It attracts top global brands like Audi, Volkswagen, Siemens, Perkins, and Monginis. With smart city infrastructure, world-class facilities, and sustainable planning, Shendra MIDC & AURIC are emerging as Maharashtra’s most advanced hubs for manufacturing, logistics, and innovation.

🔷 Waluj MIDC

Spanning 1,298 hectares, Waluj MIDC is a major industrial township home to giants such as Bajaj Auto, Coca-Cola, Goodyear, Endurance Group, Siemens, Johnson & Johnson, Mylan, Cosmo Films, and Carlsberg. Its strategic location and transport connectivity make it a cornerstone of the region's industrial landscape. This zone supports automotive, FMCG, pharmaceuticals, and heavy engineering sectors, offering a skilled workforce and mature ecosystem.

🔷 Chikalthana MIDC & Paithan MIDC

These are well-established industrial areas with a diverse mix of industries. Chikalthana MIDC is conveniently located near the airport and city center, making it logistically advantageous for businesses. It hosts auto component firms and leading pharmaceutical names like Wockhardt and Glenmark. Paithan MIDC, located close to the historic town of Paithan, provides a quieter and cost-effective industrial setting. Both MIDCs contribute significantly to regional growth and house a robust base of SMEs and legacy industries.
